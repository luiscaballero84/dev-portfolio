We've simplified the planning process, giving our guests more time to savor their stay and create lasting memories.

Resolving guest information issues

As a hospitality-tech company, Raus offers city dwellers the opportunity to escape the fast-paced urban life and find comfort in the serenity of nature.

At Raus, we live by the motto #time_well_spent. However, we realized that our guests were investing a lot of time organizing their trips instead of fully enjoying their stay with us.

To address this, we created the Raus Companion: a digital guidebook that serves as a central hub for our guests needs. It provides essential information such as directions, check-in/out times, house rules, local recommendations, and more.

A recurring problem

We noticed that our guests were struggling to find important information about their trips in an easy way. As a result, they were contacting our Guest Experience team more frequently than desired, causing frustration for both sides.

We already knew the solution to this problem, but in order to dive deep into the issue, we organized a workshop with key stakeholders. We wanted to identify and understand better the pain points experienced by our guests and ourselves.

With the insights from the workshop, we created a product roadmap. Our strategy was to launch a minimal viable product (MVP) with essential features first. This approach allowed us to start quickly, test the product in selected cabins, and make improvements based on guest feedback.

New features and implementation across all cabins

After a few weeks since the MVP launch, we received valuable feedback and had enough time to make improvements and incorporate the missing features. We introduced a new key feature for purchasing extras and successfully implemented Raus Companion in all cabins.

Raus Companion is currently available in every cabin, and it has greatly improved our guests' stay experience.
