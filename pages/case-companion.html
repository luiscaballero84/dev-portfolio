Introduction

Raus, a hospitality-tech company, offers urban residents a break from the fast-paced city life in calm cabins amidst nature.

In this project, we developed the "Raus Companion", a digital guidebook web-based app, to simplify trip planning, providing a one-stop solution to answer all our guests‚Äò questions.

My role

I served as the product designer for this project and collaborated with 2 developers, (Anna and Fabian). Given our small team, Fabian also undertook the role of the product manager.
